Right Across Opportunity. Right at The Heart

Virat NRI Avenue is an iconic commercial development that brings a new wave of business opportunity and lifestyle experience to Jaipur’s fastest-growing corridor – Mahal Road, Jagatpura. Conceptualized and developed by the reputed Virat Group, this project is a strategic blend of modern infrastructure, smart investment potential, and long-term growth.

Spread across a prime location with excellent road connectivity and dense catchment, Virat NRI Avenue is designed to become a commercial landmark, catering to NRIs, investors, entrepreneurs, and aspiring business owners.

Frequently Asked Questions

The project is located adjoining Jaipur Chaupati, Jagatpura, a prime area in Jaipur with excellent connectivity and high commercial potential.

Virat NRI Avenue is a mixed-use commercial development featuring:
  • Offices – 400 to 500 sq. ft.
  • Shops – 150 to 300 sq. ft.
  • Showrooms – 6200 to 6400 sq. ft.
  • Studio Apartments – 431 to 460 sq. ft.
  • Food Kiosks – 250 sq. ft.
  • • Gaming Zone – 1300 to 2100 sq. ft.

The building is designed as G+11 floors, with a well-planned vertical distribution of commercial and residential spaces.

  • Lower Ground – Shops
  • Ground Floor – Showrooms
  • Upper Ground – Shops
  • 2nd Floor – Food Court & Gaming Zone
  • 3rd to 5th Floor – Offices
  • 6th to 11th Floor – Studio Apartments

The project features two-level basement multilevel parking, providing sufficient space for both customers and residents.
